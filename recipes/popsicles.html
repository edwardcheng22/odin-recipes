Cherry Limeade Vodka Popsicles

Description

These vodka popsicles are super simple with three ingredientsâ€”just limeade, cherry vodka, and fresh cherries. These will definitely be the talk among the adults.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups limeade
  • 1 cup cherry vodka
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cherries

Steps

  1. Stir together limeade, cherry vodka, and cherries. Pour into ice pop molds and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
